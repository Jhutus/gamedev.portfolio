Projects

Tiempos Pasados

Letting go of the past has never been so visual. In this game, the result of my second 2-day game jam, you play as a character seeking to leave behind painful memories. As you activate monuments and release those burdens, a dark fog lifts, revealing a clearer world. But beware, you only have one minute to solve each challenge. Can you let go of what holds you back and move forward?

Paraexit

What would you do to escape a laboratory if you were a parasite? In this game, created during my first 3-day game jam, you become a parasite that must infect doctors and patients to take on their appearance and personality, solving challenges and opening doors to escape. Can you deceive everyone and make it out alive? Find out!

Suspiro

An action-packed, time-based game where you must capture your enemies before the clock runs out. Featuring 3D movement with 2D assets, Suspiro creates a unique 2.5D aesthetic that blends depth and dynamism in every match. As the game's developer and artist, I designed the mechanics to create a fast-paced and immersive experience.

Pachinko

A custom take on the classic Pachinko game, featuring original artwork. In this 2D game, balls drop from the top of the screen, and your goal is to catch them in a moving box that loops from left to right. Precision and quick reflexes are essential to score points. As the game's developer and visual artist, I created all the graphics and programmed the mechanics to enhance the challenge.

Piso es Lava

My first venture into 3D development with Unity. This experimental project explores physics, collisions, and object properties in a virtual space. It served as a hands-on exercise to understand how elements interact in a 3D environment. As the designer, developer, and technical artist, I worked on the mechanics, physics, and overall structure of the game.
